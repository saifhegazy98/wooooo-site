Terms of Use

Last updated 10 July 2026
WOOOOO works because of one unspoken agreement: everyone here is trying to be a little kinder. These are the simple rules that keep it that way.

Using WOOOOO

By using the WOOOOO app you agree to these terms. WOOOOO is a free, anonymous place to share moments and send encouragement. You must be 17 or older to use it.

The community rules

The only thing you can send another person is warmth. There are no comments, no replies, and no profiles — so the main way to cause harm is through what you post. So:

  • Be real, be kind. Wins, hard days, quiet confessions — all welcome.
  • No advice-giving, no selling, no spam, no links.
  • No naming or targeting real people.

Zero tolerance for objectionable content

There is no tolerance for content or behaviour that is abusive, harassing, hateful, threatening, sexually explicit, promotes self-harm to others, or is otherwise objectionable or illegal. Posts are screened automatically before they appear, and:

  • Anyone can report a moment from the app. Reported content is removed from the community immediately while we review it.
  • We review reports promptly (within 24 hours) and remove content that breaks these rules.
  • We may block access for anyone who abuses the community.

By posting, you agree not to submit objectionable content, and you accept that we may remove anything and restrict access at our discretion to keep people safe.

Your content

Your words are yours. By posting to the community you give us permission to display them anonymously within WOOOOO. Vents are shown publicly for 24 hours and then kept only in your own private timeline.

Not a crisis or medical service

WOOOOO offers human encouragement, not professional, medical, or crisis care. It is not a substitute for a doctor, therapist, or emergency services. If you or someone else may be in danger, contact your local emergency number or a crisis line right away (in the US, call or text 988). Where we can, we'll surface real help alongside encouragement.

Ending use
